Research interest

- I'm interested in quantum information and entanglement entropy. With Dr. Mark Mezei at Stonybrook, I'm working on time evolution of entanglment entropy after global quench through the bulk calculation. With Dr. Clement Berthiere at Peking University, I'm working on the calculation of reflected entropy in Lifshitz field theory and Chern-Simons theory. I'm also interested in black-hole information problem.(who are not?)

- I'm interested in the holographic understanding of squashed AdS geoetries. With Prof. Nikolay Bobev at KU Leuven, we're investigating the bulk and boundary behavior of a squashed AdS8 geometry with SO(5)*SO(3) isometry.

- I'm interested in calculating gravitational waves emitted from black hole mergers with large mass ratios.
